Parmesan Chopped Salad

We're starting off the blog with a delicious and refreshing salad. This recipe is so easy to make and can be paired with any main dish to create the perfect side.

Ingredients:

  • Romaine lettuce: 1 to 2 hearts
  • Shallots: 2, thinly sliced
  • Lemons: zest and juice
  • Extra virgin olive oil: 1/4 cup
  • Salt and pepper: 1/2 teaspoons
  • Crushed red pepper flakes: 1/4 teaspoon, optional
  • Paremesan cheese: 1 cup finely grated
