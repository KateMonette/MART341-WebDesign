Simple and Fresh Recipes!

First stop: KAUAI

We're starting off the blog with a trip to Kauai.

Ingredients:

  • Romaine lettuce: 1 to 2 hearts
  • Shallots: 2, thinly sliced
  • Lemons: zest and juice
  • Extra virgin olive oil: 1/4 cup
  • Salt and pepper: 1/2 teaspoons
  • Crushed red pepper flakes: 1/4 teaspoon, optional
  • Paremesan cheese: 1 cup finely grated

Instructions:

  1. To make the parmesan chopped salad: Chop the romaine lettuce with a sharp knife, put in your salad spinner or a large bowl, rinse and drain very well, or pat dry.
  2. Add the lettuce, shallots, parmesan, and lemon zest to a large salad bowl. Combine kosher salt, black pepper, lemon juice, and olive oil in a jar and pour over the salad. Toss the parmesan salad to combine before serving. Enjoy!
